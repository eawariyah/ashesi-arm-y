About Us

Welcome to the official website of the Arm-y Student Club at Ashesi University!

Established in 2022, our clubs has a unique role: to foster a community of students from diverse disciplines who share a common passion for technology and innovation. We are proud to be affiliated with the larger Advanced RISC Machines (ARM) organization, which is renowned for its cutting-edge technology solutions. Our club operates as a feature of the newly established (E³)NGAGE labs in Ghana, a part of ARM's initiative to expand its African presence.

What we do?

Our club is dedicated to making ARM processors and designs accessible to everyone. We strive to close the gap between the commonly perceived complex ARM processors and designs to the basic level. Our goal is to help students understand the fundamentals and come up with experimental designs that increase the growth of ARM-based user groups, develop networks, and boost interest and education to help bring about innovation and designs of the future which would benefit us all. We provide our members with hands-on experience, that is, access to cutting-edge Arm-based microcontrollers, and opportunities to collaborate on projects. By doing so, we hope to inspire the next generation of innovators and contribute to the growth of the ARM ecosystem.

Some of the key activities and initiatives that our club focuses on include:

  • Embedded Learning Challenges: We provide opportunities for our members to learn the fundamentals, take part in various embedded learning challenges, and participate in a programmes that enable students to work together on Arm-based projects and gain practical experience with Arm-based microcontrollers (MCUs) and Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA's).
  • Aritficial Intelligence, Computer Vision and Machine Learning including TinyML: We support the application and growth of AI, machine learning, and importantly TinyML in a dynamic environment, realising their enormous potential in a variety of uses and applications. By utilising the thriving TinyML ecosystem, we provide our members with cooperative training programmes and a wealth of knowledge resources. Through these initiatives, we help our members fully realise TinyML's transformative potential, giving them the tools they need to come up with original ideas and have a positive influence on the rapidly evolving AI and machine learning industries.
  • Internet of things (IOT): Aside from our club's focus on ARM processors and designs, while also acknowledging the transformative potential of the Internet of Things (IoT). Through various activities and initiatives, we empower members to explore IoT's integration with ARM-based systems. We aim to create and implement IoT solutions that enhance user experiences, streamline processes, and drive technological advancements. By emphasizing the interconnectedness of devices and networks, we encourage exploration in areas like smart homes, industrial automation, healthcare, and environmental monitoring. With a focus on security and privacy, we promote robust measures and ethical practices for responsible IoT deployments.
