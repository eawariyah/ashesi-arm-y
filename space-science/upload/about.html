About Us

Welcome to the official website of the Arm Student Club at Ashesi University!

Established in 2022, our clubs has a unique role: to foster a vibrant community of students from diverse disciplines who share a common passion for technology and innovation. We are proud to be affiliated with the larger Advanced RISC Machines (ARM) organization, which is renowned for its cutting-edge technology solutions. Our club operates as a feature of the newly established (E³)NGAGE labs in Ghana, a part of ARM's initiative to expand its African presence.

What we do?

Our club is dedicated to making ARM processors and designs more accessible to everyone. We strive to bridge the gap between the often thought of difficult to understand and too far to reach ARM processors and designs to the basic level. Our goal is to help students understand the fundamentals and come up with experimental designs that increase the growth of ARM-based user groups, developer networks, and boost interest and education to help bring about innovation and designs of the future which would benefit us all. We provide our members with hands-on experience, *access to cutting-edge Arm-based microcontrollers [both theoretically and physically], and opportunities to collaborate on projects. By doing so, we hope to inspire the next generation of innovators and contribute to the growth of the ARM ecosystem

Some of the key activities and initiatives that our club focuses on include:

  • Embedded Learning Challenge: We offer our members the chance to participate in the Embedded Learning Challenge, an initiative that enables engineers to gain hands-on experience with Arm-based microcontrollers (MCUs) and collaborate with like-minded enthusiasts on Arm-based projects and learning curriculum from Arm Education online-sales-marketing.com.
  • TinyML: We actively support the growth of TinyML communities across Africa, leveraging the rich ecosystem of TinyML developer groups and providing tools, training, and knowledge resources to our members online-sales-marketing.com.
  • Collaboration with Partners: We work closely with key Arm technology partners, such as Oracle, Google, and AWS, to support the growth of technology in Africa. Our partnerships with these industry leaders enable us to attract open-source developers, researchers, and industry partners to run their compute workloads on Arm and contribute to the Arm ecosystem online-sales-marketing.com.
  • Education and Outreach: We are committed to supporting the Arm School Program for Young Learners, which delivers world-class STEM exposure to students across Africa. Our club also actively supports the integration of Arm Education initiatives into K-12 schools and universities in Ghana online-sales-marketing.com. Community Engagement: We work closely with organizations such as Makerspace Ghana and the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) to support technology-driven learning and skills development, particularly in the K-12 sector
